About Us


Throughout India, pedestrians are among the worst affected by traffic accidents, according to 2018 national data on traffic accidents. In Karnataka itself, there were more than 1,500 pedestrians killed in a fatal road accidents last year, and country-wide, there was a whopping 22,656 pedestrians killed in a road accident in the same period. So, we come up with the idea Garud which monitor and improve pedestrian safety infrastructure. It takes IP Addresses of nearby CCTV cameras and detect the defaulter.
